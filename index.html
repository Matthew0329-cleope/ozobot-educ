About Ozobot & Evollve Inc.

Ozobot is a product of Evollve Inc., a company dedicated to creating innovative educational tools that make learning to code fun and accessible. The company is best known for its small, programmable robots designed to teach coding to children and adults.

Our Founder

Ozobot and Evollve Inc. were founded by Nader Hamda, an entrepreneur with a passion for education and technology. His vision was to create a hands-on, engaging way for people of all ages to learn about coding and robotics.

The Vision Behind Ozobot

Nader Hamda's vision for Ozobot was to break down the barriers to learning to code. He recognized that traditional coding education could be intimidating and abstract, especially for young learners. Ozobot was designed to be:

  1. Accessible: Easy to use for beginners, with a low entry barrier.
  2. Engaging: Fun and interactive, to keep learners motivated.
  3. Versatile: Capable of both screen-free and screen-based coding.
  4. Educational: A tool for teaching fundamental coding concepts and computational thinking.

Key Innovations

Dual Coding

Ozobot's ability to be programmed with both color codes and block-based programming provides a unique and flexible learning experience.

Educational Focus

Evollve Inc. and Nader Hamda have prioritized the use of Ozobot in educational settings, making it a valuable tool for teachers and students.
